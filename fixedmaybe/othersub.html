Cooking Tips for the Perfect Mac n Cheese

  • Use freshly grated cheese – It melts better and tastes fresher than pre-shredded cheese.
  • Don’t overcook the pasta – Slightly undercook it so it doesn’t get mushy after baking.
  • Add a little mustard powder – It enhances the cheese flavor without making it taste like mustard.
  • Use a mix of cheeses – Blend sharp cheddar with creamy cheeses like mozzarella or gouda for more flavor.
  • Top it off – Add breadcrumbs or crushed crackers for a crunchy topping before baking.
