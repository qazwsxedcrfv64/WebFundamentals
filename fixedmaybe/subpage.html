Our Favorite Mac n Cheese Recipes

Classic Baked Mac n Cheese

Ingredients: Elbow pasta, cheddar cheese, milk, butter, flour, breadcrumbs.

Instructions: Make a cheese sauce with butter, flour, milk, and cheddar. Mix with cooked pasta, top with breadcrumbs, and bake at 375°F for 25 minutes.

Bacon Mac n Cheese

Ingredients: Pasta, sharp cheddar, cooked bacon, heavy cream, butter, flour, pepper.

Instructions: Stir crumbled bacon into the cheese sauce, combine with pasta, and bake until golden. Add extra bacon on top before serving.

Spicy Jalapeño Mac n Cheese

Ingredients: Pasta, pepper jack cheese, cream cheese, jalapeños, milk, butter, garlic.

Instructions: Sauté jalapeños and garlic in butter. Mix with creamy cheese sauce, combine with pasta, and bake for 20–25 minutes.
